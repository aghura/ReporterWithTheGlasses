Whispers

There is something about this place
This little village called Amherst
Its immortal poets whisper in my ears
Poetry, romance, heartache
They make me pen it down
“I’m scared I’m scared,” I say
I want to write more
What if it’s the peak of my creativity?
The last supper with my poems?
And they laugh back and prove me wrong anyways.

For all those who bring color into my black and white life.
