Rusty Brain

Inspiration: 10th grade Maths Board Exam.

Page 1 to 100 open,
I’m told to study study,
I’m going bonkers growing old and old.
This learning is a killing,
Torture that is billing,
My rusty brain is fillin’,
But it will not listen anymore!

Dunking in with knowledge,
Ledgering for college,
It’s bugging and it’s drumming,
My cerebrum is mugging,
Jumbling in its cranium.

The light is blinding,
It’s source I’m finding,
My mind is dying,
It’s huffing and it’s puffing,
Singing and tinkling,
The alphabets dwindling,
My rusty brain is fillin’,
But it will not listen anymore!

Created with awesomeness on iPad, MacBook, and Adobe.
For all those who bring color into my black and white life.
