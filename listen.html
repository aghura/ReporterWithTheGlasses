Listen!! • 39 words • March 3, 2022

Listen!!

For a friend who wouldn’t listen.

Listen listen,
My thoughts are bustling,
In my mind they’re rustling,
It’s clear they want an audience right now.

They burble to be voiced,
Waken to be heard,
Aashi drop dead your work,
And listen to my poetic bluebird.

Update: She still didn’t listen.

For all those who bring color into my black and white life.
